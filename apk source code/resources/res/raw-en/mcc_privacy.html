Privacy policy for your Monsieur Cuisine connect

Thank you for your interest in data protection when using your Monsieur Cuisine connect kitchen appliance ("Monsieur Cuisine connect", "MCC").

The following data protection notice is designed to inform you about how and to what extent your personal data is potentially processed when you use your MCC. Personal data is information that identifies you or could identify you directly or indirectly. The statutory basis for data protection is, in particular, the EU and UK General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

1                Data processing in offline mode

Purposes of the processing/legal bases:

We only process your personal data when your MCC is connected to a wireless network (WIFI). If it is not connected to a wireless network, we only store some technical information locally on your MCC (the following data in particular: operating system and app version of the built-in tablet, operating parameters of the cooking functions, motor and heat data, operating times, system time, error information, last notification, lid open times, scale calibrated).

If you send or hand over your appliance to Lidl customer service for maintenance or repair, we also collect and use information obtained from error diagnostics to pursue our legitimate interest in the technical development of the MCC and in improving our products and services (e.g., recipes).

Storage time/criteria for determining storage time:

If we return your appliance to you after repair instead of replacing it, your appliance will continue to process your data. If we read or store the data on your appliance while performing error diagnostics, we will delete the data after the warranty periods have expired (after 5 years at the latest).

With respect to data processing for the purposes of troubleshooting, please also note the privacy policies of the Lidl company where you purchased your appliance or where you sent your device for repair (e.g., at https://www.lidl.de/c/datenschutz/s10007528).

2                Data processing in online mode

As soon as you connect your MCC to a wireless network and, where applicable, log in to your customer account, we will generally only process data that is necessary to use the appliance and its functions (sections 2.1 and 2.2). We only process any additional data if we have a legitimate interest (section 2.3) or you have consented to this (section 2.4).

2.1            Operating your MCC

Purposes of the processing/legal bases:

If you connect your MCC to a wireless network, we will process data relating to your MCC and your internet connection in order to provide you with basic functions such as downloading the recipes you select. We also process this data to update the software on your MCC in the interest of ensuring error-free operation and, where necessary, to install new functions and default recipes. The following specific data is processed in particular: date/time and content of the request, IP address, operating system and app version of the in-built tablet, version of the privacy policy.

This processing is required to enable us to fulfil our contract with you.

Storage time/criteria for determining storage time:

We store the data used to offer you the functions of your MCC for a maximum of 90 days.

2.2            Registering and using your Monsieur Cuisine account

Purposes of the processing/legal bases:

You can use your Monsieur Cuisine connect kitchen appliance to create a user account and subsequently use this account to log in to your appliance to use certain additional features (e.g., to select your favorite recipes). You can also synchronise your account on your appliance, your app, and the website, where applicable.

After registering your account, you will be sent an activation link which you can use to activate your account and create a password. To complete the registration process, your e-mail address provided in this context must be validated and registration is then confirmed by us.

During the registration process, the following data will be collected:

  • pseudonym ("nickname");
  • e-mail address;
  • password and
  • automatically generated user ID.

The data provided by you during the registration process must be correct. Third party data may not be used.

In addition, you can complete your profile with the following voluntary information:

  • profile picture;
  • appliance model;
  • own recipes (website only);
  • pictures of own recipes (website only) and
  • comments on recipes.

 

We also use your e-mail address to

  • assist you with any account problems you may encounter, and
  • inform you about changes to our Terms of Use, or, where necessary, changes to this privacy policy.

This processing is required to enable us to fulfil our contract with you.

Recipients/categories of recipient:

We use service providers to process your data. These service providers provide us with storage capacity, database systems, technical support, and marketing and technical consultancy. All of our processors are carefully selected, audited by us and bound by contract.

Your list of favorite recipes can only be viewed by yourself. The recipes you create and upload, along with any comments you add, can be viewed by third parties together with your nickname and, if applicable,your profile picture.

 

 

Storage time/criteria for determining storage time:

We will delete your data if you request the deletion of your Monsieur Cuisine account. Any comments and recipes you may have uploaded to date will then be anonymised and can continue to be viewed by visitors to the website and other users of the MC app. If you wish to have the comments and recipes you have uploaded deleted completely, please inform us of this in a separate request to our customer service (section 3). We will then completely delete your recipes and comments.

You can also delete all of your user data stored locally on your MCC by resetting your appliance to factory settings ("factory reset"). To reset your appliance, go to "Settings" and click on "Factory settings" and then "Reset to factory settings".

2.3            Determining your country of residence

Purposes of the processing/legal bases:

We can send you pop-up messages on your MCC. We record and analyze your IP address so that we can localize the messages as best as possible. In doing so, it is not possible for us to draw any specific conclusions about you or your exact location. We are only able to determine the country in which you reside.

The legal basis for processing your IP address is Article 6(1)(f) GDPR. Our (shared) legitimate interest in this data processing arises from the objective of displaying messages that are localized to the country in which you reside.

Storage time/criteria for determining storage time:

Your IP address will be deleted at the latest after the campaign that gave rise to our messages ends.

2.4            Analysis of how you use our services

Purposes of the processing/legal bases:

If you have consented to us analysing how you use your MCC, we will process the following information in particular to check and further develop the functions of your MCC, to optimise the recipes provided, if necessary adapt the list of recipes depending on the habits of users in your region, and to efficiently and successfully provide support, repair and maintenance services for your MCC: information on your cooking and other use of your MCC (e.g., functions used per recipe, active time per recipe step, specific MCC functions used, your appliance settings).

If you have given consent for data processing on one or more occasions, we store the date stamp and fact that you have given your consent together with your appliance ID. This allows us to meet our legal obligation to demonstrate the lawfulness of the data processing based on consentIf you do not give your consent, we store information to the effect that you have not consented to data processing.

Storage time/criteria for determining storage time:

If you have consented to the processing of your data onhow you use your MCC, we store this data on the basis of your consent for a maximum of 7 days following collection. After this period, your data will be anonymised and aggregated as statistical reports before we will permanently delete the data 6 months later.

We store data demonstrating your consent for a maximum of 24 months after we have ceased processing your personal data based on your consent.

2.5            Recipients/categories of recipients

In some cases we use service providers to process your data. They are bound by our instructions and audited by us prior to data processing and thereafter on a regular basis. Such providers never process your personal data for their own purposes.

In this context we share your data with companies who support us in the following areas:

o storage capacity, database systems

o technical support, content editing and user account management

If your personal data is processed in a state outside of the European Economic Area, we ensure strict compliance with the provisions of the GDPR regarding transfers to such third countries (in particular by means of standard data protection clauses). Such data access is currently only possible from the United States and Hong Kong, and in some cases only after separate approval has been given by one of our service providers in the EU.

3                Your rights as the data subject

You have the right to receive, upon request, information about the personal data stored with us about you, free of charge in accordance with Art. 15 GDPR. This includes in particular, the purpose, the categories of personal data that we process, the recipients or the categories of recipients to whom the personal data concerned have been disclosedIn addition, you have the right to have incorrect data corrected; or you can have your personal data deleted and restrict the processing or transmission.   

In cases where data is processed based on Article 6(1)(e) or (f) GDPR and/or for the purposes of direct advertising, you have the right to object to processing

If you have given your consent on one or more occasion, you may withdraw any such consent at any time with effect for the future.

If you wish to exercise this right or have any questions about the collection, processing and use of your personal data, please do not hesitate to contact us at any time at dataprotection-mc@lidl.co.uk.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the relevant supervisory authority.

4                No obligation to provide data

You are under no obligation to provide personal data. However, not providing the data stored in your account and not establishing an internet connection will restrict available functions of your MCC (such as downloading new recipes or software updates). In addition, we may – without your consent for data processing – present you with a small number of products and recipes that may potentially be of interest to you.

5                Amendments to the privacy policy

Changes in the law or in circumstances relating to data processing in the context of using the MCC may necessitate changes to this privacy policy. If changes are made to the purposes of collecting, processing or using your personal data or the identity of the controller and categories of recipients, we will notify you of these and, where necessary, ask for your consent.

The Lidl Stiftung & Co. KG data protection officer can be contacted at the above address (c/o data protection officer – please add the prefix: "zu Händen des Datenschutzbeauftragten") or the address provided in section 3.
